All About Women and technology

'Akira' is a Japanese word that means 'energy' and 'intelligence'.

The biggest driving force to starting Akirachix was the fact that they noticed a huge gap in the presence of women within the technology workplace, school, and even at the iHub as it was starting up. The situation that aroused their interest even more was noticing that there were just a handful of women in the room during the launch of Nairobiâ€™s innovation Hub. These ladies therefore decided to start a forum where women in the technology industry could get to meet each other, share their stories and encourage other girls to take up careers that are technology related..

The Milestones ofAkirachix

Akirachix hopes to expand its training program to various parts of the country in the near future.

It aims at increasing more women in technology industry but more so focuses on young girls from unstable background. Apart from this they also aspire to have their own technology School and in line with this they want to build a school in honor of one of its alumni who perish in a fire that consumed half of deep sea slum in December , 2013 name was Maureen Chepkwony, she was passionate about children and her dream was to have a school to host all those who were unable to go to school.
